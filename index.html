Easy Terms to Know

Autoimmune

When the body's protective "guards" get confused and attack their own home instead of germs.

Collagen

The "glue" of the body. In the right amount, it keeps us strong. In too much, it makes us stiff.

Fibrosis

The process of skin becoming thick or scarred. Like a wound that keeps "healing" even after it's closed.

Systemic

When the hardening moves from the outside skin to the inside organs, like the lungs or heart.
